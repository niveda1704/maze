# Neon Flow Maze

**Neon Flow Maze** is a browser-based skill game where you guide a glowing neon orb through intricate mazes using smooth mouse or touch controls.

## How to Run

###  Double Click 
1. Navigate to the folder where you saved `maze.html`.
2. **Double-click** the file.
3. It will open automatically in your default web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, etc.).

##  How to Play

1. **Start Game**: Click the "START GAME" button on the overlay screen.
2. **Controls**: 
   *   **Mouse**: Click and hold anywhere on the screen. The orb will follow your cursor.
   *   **Touch**: Touch and hold the screen. The orb will follow your finger.
3. **Objective**: Guide the **Blue Neon Orb** through the maze to reach the **Red Glowing Zone**.
4. **Rules**: 
   *   Do **NOT** touch the dark walls.
   *   If you hit a wall, you stop moving.
   *   Complete the level to advance to the next harder maze.
5. **Audio**: Click once on the page to ensure sound effects are enabled (browser policy requires interaction).

## Features
*   **Smooth Physics**: Fluid movement system; the orb glides towards your input.
*   **Neon Aesthetic**: Glowing visuals, particle trails, and dynamic lighting effects.
*   **Infinite Levels**: Levels 1-2 are handcrafted tutorials. Level 3+ are procedurally generated and get progressively harder.
*   **Cross-Platform**: Works with Mouse on Desktop and Touch on Mobile/Tablet.

##  Technology
Built with **Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.
*   **Rendering**: HTML5 Canvas for high-performance graphics.
*   **Audio**: Web Audio API for synthesized sound effects 
*   **Single File**: The entire game is contained within `maze.html` for easy sharing.
